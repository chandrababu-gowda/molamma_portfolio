Hello

I am Dr Molamma, with over 20 years of experience in food and material sciences research, teaching and industry.

With BSc in Chemistry and a BSc Tech in Food Science & Technology from the prestigious university, Institute of Chemical Technology (or UDCT) in Mumbai, India, I entered the perfumery and flavour industry. Further, acquired a scholarship to do PhD in Chemistry from the National University of Singapore in Singapore.

An educator by profession who train, mentor and guide students & early-career individuals in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths), I have trained more than 10 post-graduate students from across various countries such as Germany, China, Iran, Argentina and Singapore. Recognized as a scientific mentor for my ability to mentor & guide students to their higher motivation bringing the best learning outcome.

Areas where I have contributed comprises of a multitude of science and engineering subjects including the food science, regenerative medicine, nanotechnology, food waste valorisation, vegan meat & fish development, thus contributed highly to the scientific community in the last few years.

Sharing is caring. Let us CONNECT to LEARN and GROW a community of science and engineering leaders, with passion, courage and collaboration.

I am keen on nurturing talents with emphasis on kindness, growth, helping to overcome fear/challenges, being their true self projecting authenticity, thus aligning their inner and outer worlds.
