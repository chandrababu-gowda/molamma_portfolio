Book review

Reading helps to develop our mind, and it enhances your vocabulary and communication skills. I am constantly preparing a reading list, and when the author name or book name come repeatedly in front of me, then I know it's time to hit that book!

These are a few self-development books that helped me grow as a person.

  • The artist's way by Julia Cameron
  • Feel the fear and do it anyway by Susan Jeffers
  • Mind set by Carol Dweck
  • A New Earth by Eckart Tolle
  • The courage to be disliked by I Kishimi & F Koga
  • The monk who sold his Ferrari by Robin Sharma
  • Quiet by Susan Cain

Reading allowed me to review the essence of the book, and it was summarized and compiled as a blog. Putting what we learned into practice is something we have to practice. In that sense, re-reading the books can help. Self-awareness is the first step, and it allow one to accept oneself, be present or bring back to the 'now', step into your fears, and be a light to others. Happiness is peace. Practice the life SAVERS (Silence, Affirmations, Visualization, Exercise, Reading, Scribing) as said by Hal Elrod.
