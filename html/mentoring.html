Mentoring

Skilled and passionate mentor to girls & women across the globe, from India, Malaysia, Korea, Australia, Canada and Singapore.

Mentoring provide an accelerated route for development. I started this with the idea of giving back to the society, thus passing my knowledge to someone who can thus have a better approach to challenges in their own studies or career journey.

The method of mentoring I followed mainly involved the following aspects: Open conversations, goal oriented, enumerated development strategies including language proficiency, communication skills/ style, writing and articulation, career planning, self-awareness, thus influencing mentees in their preparedness to have a globally impactful and purposeful life journey.

I do get different perspective and learn from my mentees throughout this process.
